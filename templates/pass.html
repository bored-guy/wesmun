2011年第一届汇文模拟联合国大会

2011年第一届会议邀请到来自全国15个省、市、自治区25所学校的近280名代表参会。大会共设置六个委员会，包括主新闻中心，两个中文会场——世界粮食峰会、世界卫生大会，与三个英文会场——Security council Human Rights Council, United Nations Environmental Program

2011 – 6 committees along with 1 Main Press Center were held, involving nearly 280 delegates from 25 renowned high schools across the country. The conference attached importance to worldwide issues concerning humankind’s sustainability. The first session consists of two Chinese committees, three English committees and a special bilingual committee including World Food Summit, the World Health Assembly, Council Human, Rights Council United, Nations Environmental Program Security and the Main Press Center
